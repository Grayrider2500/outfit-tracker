## Description (draft)

**Dressed** is a **local-first** closet organizer: your clothes, your outfits, your data on your iPhone.

**Wardrobe**  
Add pieces with photos, category, size, color, and seasons. Log wears and see how often you reach for each item.

**Outfits**  
Combine pieces into saved outfits with a collage-style home screen. See **which outfits use a given piece** from item detail.

**Search & suggest**  
Filter your closet and open **Suggest outfits** for occasion-based ideas scored for balance and rotation. (Richer on-device explanations in development; release builds use the same reliable rule engine.)

**Backup & borrowable libraries**  
Export a backup archive to keep a copy of your wardrobe. Share a **.dressed-library** file of pieces you mark as **available to lend**, and import libraries from friends—all without a cloud account.

**Privacy**  
Data stays on your device unless you explicitly export or share a file.
