Dressed is your personal wardrobe companion — a private, beautiful space to catalog every piece you own, build outfit looks, track what you actually wear, and share your style with people you trust.

YOUR WARDROBE, ORGANIZED
Add every piece in your closet with a photo, color, size, category, and season tags. Browse your collection in a clean two-column grid and filter by tops, bottoms, dresses, shoes, outerwear, or accessories with a single tap.

BUILD OUTFITS YOU LOVE
Mix and match pieces from your wardrobe to create and save outfit looks. Each outfit displays as a photo collage so you can see at a glance how your pieces work together.

SHARE YOUR WARDROBE WITH FAMILY & FRIENDS
Mark items as available to lend and export them as a Wardrobe Library — a single file with photos included. Share it via AirDrop, Messages, email, or any app. Recipients open it in Dressed and browse your pieces in their own Borrowed Libraries section. Perfect for sharing with a partner, sibling, or friend with similar taste.

FIND ANYTHING FAST
Search by name, filter by category or season, and sort by recently added, most worn, or alphabetically. Your entire closet is always one tap away.

TRACK WHAT YOU WEAR
Mark items and outfits as worn to see your real favorites. Rediscover the pieces hiding in the back of your closet and make the most of everything you own.

BACKUP & RESTORE
Export your entire wardrobe as a single backup file at any time. Restore with a merge (adds new items, skips duplicates) or a full replace — your data, your control.

COMPLETELY PRIVATE
Everything lives on your device. No account required, no cloud sync, no ads, no tracking. Your wardrobe data belongs to you alone.
