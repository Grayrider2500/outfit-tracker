Dressed

Privacy Policy — Last updated May 2026

Your data stays on your device

Dressed does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers. All wardrobe items, outfit data, and photos you add are saved locally on your iPhone using Apple's SwiftData framework and your device's file system.

No accounts or sign-in required

Dressed has no user accounts, login, or registration. There is nothing to sign up for and no credentials are ever created or stored.

No analytics or tracking

Dressed does not use any analytics, crash-reporting, advertising, or third-party tracking SDKs. No usage data is collected.

Photos

Photos you attach to wardrobe items are stored only on your device. They are never uploaded to any server. Access to your photo library is requested solely to let you pick or capture clothing photos within the app.

Backup files

When you use the Backup feature, Dressed creates a JSON file on your device. You may share or save that file using iOS's standard share sheet — that action is entirely under your control. Dressed itself does not send the file anywhere.

Children's privacy

Dressed does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. Because no data is collected at all, there is nothing to distinguish.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date.
