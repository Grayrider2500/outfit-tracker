## Full description (draft)

**Dressed** helps you organize your real clothes—without accounts or the cloud. Everything stays on your device.

**Wardrobe**  
Add pieces with photos, category, size, color, and seasons. Mark what you wore and see wear history at a glance.

**Outfits**  
Build outfits from your wardrobe, browse them in a collage-style layout, and track how often you wear each look. Opening a piece shows **which saved outfits include it**.

**Search & suggest**  
Filter your closet and use **Suggest outfits** to get scored ideas for an occasion—balanced colors, rotation, and your habits. (Optional developer-only enhancement for richer explanations; store builds use strong on-device rules.)

**Backup & libraries**  
Export a backup ZIP to keep your data safe. Share a **borrowable library** file with friends so they can browse pieces you mark as available to lend—and import libraries others send you.

**Privacy**  
Your wardrobe data is stored locally. You choose when to export, share, or restore.
